Education

I am currently a sophomore in University of California, Davis studying Computer Engineering with an expected graduation year of 2020. So far I have taken Introduction to Programming (Python), Programming and Problem-Solving (C), Software Development and Object-Oriented Programming (C++ and Linux).

Why Computer Engineering?

When I was in the fifth grade, I wrote a letter to the Lego Group asking if it was possible to make their minifigures automated. They replied saying that it was impossible to have a motor that small. As a kid, I dreamed that one day I would prove them wrong.

I am studying computer engineering so one day I can inspire and bring joy to people, not only kids, with technology the same way Lego did for me.
